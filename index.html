About Me

I'm an Assistant Professor in the School of Computer Science at Georgia Tech. My current research focuses on static analysis of programming languages that expose continuous computations, which includes both probabilistic and differentiable programming languages. I am actively recruiting multiple PhD students who plan to start in Fall 2025. If you are interested in Differentiable and Probabilistic programming, please get in touch, mention my name in your application statement and apply to Georgia Tech!
